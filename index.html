About

Since its launch in 2019, Dug & Betty's Ice Creamery has been establishing itself as one of Winnipeg's latest culinary hot spots. The Parisian-inspired setting is unlike anything this side of the Prairies - home to tantalizing small batch gourmet ice cream creations such as Strawberry Honey Balsamic Pepper, Sour Cherry with Dark Chocolate, Coconut Chocolate Fudge Oreo, Ube, and Matcha.
Dug & Betty's Ice Creamery is the latest brain child of Chef Fern Kirouac. It's located in the heart of Winnipeg's French quarter, Saint-Boniface; across the street from his other culinary success, InFerno's Bistro. It was named after his cherished basset hounds (who are the unofficial ambassadors). Also named after the duo are creations like the Dashing Dug Sundae and the Bad Betty Sundae.
The animated menu also offers a wide range of lush spirits like champagne / beer floats and a bold selection of fine wines. These can be enjoyed inside the 30-seat establishment or outdoors on the patio.
